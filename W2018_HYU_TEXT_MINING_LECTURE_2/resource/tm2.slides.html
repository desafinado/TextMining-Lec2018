8. Word2Vec & Doc2Vec¶

- Word2Vec & Doc2Vec 설계¶

  • Doc2Vec :         -PV-DM(distributed memeory)                  - PV-DBOW(distributed bag of words)
  • Word2Vec :         -CBOW(continuous bag of words)                - Skip-gram

  • $c \in \mathbb{R}^{1 \cdot V} $: $word_i$ (one-hot-encoding), $k$ , $V$
  • $d \in \mathbb{R}^{1 \cdot N} $: id (one-hot-encoding), N
  • $h \in \mathbb{R}^{1 \cdot P} $: hidden layer ($= c\cdot\hat{W}$, = $d\cdot\hat{M}$), P hyper-parameter
  • $y \in \mathbb{R}^{1 \cdot V} $: output layer $ \approx \hat{U}\cdot h$
  • $\hat{W} \in \mathbb{R}^{V \cdot P} $: input layer -> hidden layer
  • $\hat{M} \in \mathbb{R}^{N \cdot P} $: input layer -> hidden layer
  • $\hat{U} \in \mathbb{R}^{P \cdot V} $: hidden layer -> output layer , $P \cdot 1$
